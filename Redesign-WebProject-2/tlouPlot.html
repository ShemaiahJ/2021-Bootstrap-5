Twenty years after a fungal infection consumed 60% of the U.S.' population and turned the victims into deformed, cannibalistic monsters. Joel is tasked with a mission of escorting Ellie to the Fireflies, a militia group that was put together to save the world, from the leader of the Fireflies to help create a cure that could possibly wipe out all of the infected. However, this task will prove to be difficult for solely Joel, Joel has been left almost permanently bitter after the death of his daughter.
